The Automated Decoration Solution

Doning your warm clothes and braving the cold is a thing of the past. InstaDeco allows you to set up your decorations once, and display them with the press of a button every holiday season. Our patented design has been featured on TV, on programs such as Shark Tank and Dragon's Den

A man installing lights in the winter

Easy Installation

InstaDeco is easy to install. Simply measure the required length, assemble the spacers to fit, and screw into place! Afterwards, all you'll need to have the swaggiest house in the neightborhood is to plug it into an outdoor outlet.

Installing decorations with InstaDeco is a breeze. With a configuration selected, just connect your decorations to the revaled hooks, and connect the power to the cable hanging from the end of the unit.

Simple Operation

InstaDeco allows you to store multiple configurations at a time. Keep one for every holiday, or different variations of the same holiday!

Our base model includes 6 different customizable options, and our upgraded model supports up to 12. Swap your configurations by pressing the numbered buttons on your system's remote.

Upgraded model remote

Smart Home Integration

Your InstaDeco system also has the support for integration with your smart home ecosystem. InstaDeco natively supports both the Google Home™ and Amazon Alexa™ systems. InstaDeco also includes support for the Matter standard.

If you're using a self-made solution or open source project that doesn't include support for Matter, we offer an API to automate InstaDeco's features.
