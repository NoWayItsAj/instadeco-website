The Automated Decoration Solution
Doning your warm clothes and braving the cold is a thing of the past. InstaDeco allows you to set up your decorations once, and display them with the press of a button every holiday season.

A man installing lights in the winter.

Easy Installation

InstaDeco is easy to install. Simply measure the required length, assemble the spacers to fit, and screw into place! Afterwards, all you'll need to have the swaggiest house in the neightborhood is to plug it into an outdoor outlet.

Installing decorations with InstaDeco is a breeze. With a configuration selected, just connect your decorations to the revaled hooks, and connect the power to the cable hanging from the end of the unit.
