Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Where can I buy InstaDeco products?

A: You can find InstaDeco products at most retailers, inside the home & garden section.


Q: I'd like to return or refund my InstaDeco product. Where do I send it?

A: Please do not return your InstaDeco product back to the retailer you purchased it from. Instead, send it to us, and we will repair, replace, or refund your product depending on it's condition. Shipping costs will be covered in most situations.


Q: What warranty is offered on InstaDeco produtcs?

A: InstaDeco offers a lifetime warranty on all of it's products. If for any reason you aren't satitisfied with your InstaDeco product, we will refund your money, and you'll be able to keep your system free of charge.


Q: What colors does InstaDeco offer?

A: InstaDeco products currently come in white, gray, black, brown, and tan. If your application requires a different color, we'd love your feedback here!
